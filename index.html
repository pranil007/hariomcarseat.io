OUR PROCESS

In Three Step Get A New Look For Your Car Interior.


Fabric Cutting



There they are visually controlled once more and then put on tables. Cutting devices featuring blades are placed on these tables to fit the maximum area possible of the leather. The table is then pressed and the cutting tools cut the parts according to the shape required to assemble the car seat.

Sewing and Stitching



Once the parts are cut they need to be prepared for sewing. To ensure that the sewing complies with the standards, the edges of the parts are skived, thus ensuring minimal grain and roughness on the areas to be sewn.

Fabric Fitting



Remove the headrest, put the front seat cover over the seat, put the mental hook through the elastic loop and fasten it on the seat frame under the seats. Put the headrest covers through the headrest, make sure it's fitting correctly and looks good, when it’s done, put it through the holes and make sure it's nice and secure.
